## Currencies

The Euro is the default currency. Amounts are rounded off after two decimal numbers. If currencies other than Euro is used during gambling/bet placing then you must make a currency conversion in accordance with the exchange rate provided by Bank of Estonia at the date of gambling.

## Player Protection

The content on Player Protection assists players in gaming responsibly. You must:

- Ensure that a person who has no right to participate in a game, does not gamble
- Ensure that a person who has been punished for a criminal offence is not tasked with organising gambling, making decisions on the right to participate in gambling or carrying out supervision of gambling
- Ensure that a person under 21 years of age is not tasked with conducting a game of chance
- Ensure that a clear warning is presented to a player regarding the addictive nature of gambling and that a reference is made to the contact details of organisations that assist gambling addicts
